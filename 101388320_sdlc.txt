DevOps plays a critical role in modern software development, focusing on the collaboration between development and operations teams to improve the speed, quality, and efficiency of software delivery.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1: Improves Collaboration and Communication skills
DevOps bridges the gap between development (Dev) and operations (Ops), therefore inadvertently improves the collaboration and Communication skills of an indivdual.

2: Faster Time to Market
DevOps practices, such as Continuous Integration (CI) and Continuous Deployment (CD), automate many stages of the software development process, reducing the time needed to release new apps and features.

3: Efficient Use of Resources
By automating repetitive tasks such as testing, deployment, DevOps frees up the development and operations teams to focus on more on technical tasks instead
