George Brown College is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It offers a variety of programs including full-time and part-time, diploma, certificate, and degree courses in a range of subjects such as technology, arts, business, and health sciences.
George Brown College was chartered in 1966 by the government of Ontario and opened the next year.
