The COMP 3104 course introduces students to the principles and practices of DevOps, a methodology that bridges the gap between software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops).

Learning Objectives:

1: DevOps Fundamentals
Understand the DevOps principles, and practices.

2: Version Control Systems
Gain proficiency in using Git for version control.

Course Format:
1: Labs --  Attend practical lab sessions to reinforce knowledge with real-world applications.
2: Group Projects -- Collaborate on group assignments that imitate real-life DevOps scenarios.
3: Assessments -- Performance evaluated through quizzes, assignments, MidTerm and Final Exams
